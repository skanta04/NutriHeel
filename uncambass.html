UNC-CH Mental Health Ambassadors

About

The mission of UNC Mental Health Ambassadors (MHA) is to promote conversation around mental health, destigmatize mental illness, facilitate support networks, and present educative programming on the campus of UNC-Chapel Hill. MHA members receive training on how to support peers struggling with mental health and work in committees to promote mental health at UNC through events, resource dissemination, and community-building.

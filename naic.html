NAMI at UNC Chapel Hill

About

The National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI) at UNC-Chapel Hill is a part of the larger NAMI organization, which is dedicated to mental health support, education, and advocacy. NAMI at UNC focuses on raising awareness, providing support, and promoting education about mental health issues. This includes supporting individuals living with mental illness as well as their friends and family members.

Counseling and Psychological Services

About

CAPS are dedicated centers that offer counseling and psychological support services tailored to the unique challenges and stressors faced by students during their academic journey. The primary focus of CAPS is to provide a safe and confidential space where students can explore and address a variety of mental health concerns. This can include academic stress, relationship issues, anxiety, depression, and other challenges commonly experienced in a university setting.

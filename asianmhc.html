Asian Mental Health Collective

About

The Asian Mental Health Collective (AMHC) is dedicated to destigmatize mental health within the Asian community, recognizing and addressing unique challenges and cultural nuances. AMHC facilitates access to Asian therapists in the United States and Canada, connecting individuals with mental health professionals who share their cultural background. This approach aims to bridge the gap between traditional Asian values and the Western mental health framework.

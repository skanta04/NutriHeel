SAATH at UNC-CH

About

SAATH (South Asian Alliance for Mental Health) is a dedicated organization committed to increasing access to mental health resources within the South Asian community through a multifaceted approach encompassing education, activism, and storytelling. Recognizing the unique cultural nuances and challenges that individuals of South Asian descent may face in addressing mental health, SAATH plays a crucial role in bridging gaps and fostering a more supportive environment.
